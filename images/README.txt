Put your photos in this folder.

Recommended:
  - hero.jpg          (used for the link preview when shared)
  - Any gallery photos, e.g. cart-1.jpg, beach-day.jpg, build-1.jpg

To show a real photo in the gallery, open index.html, find the
"gallery" section, and replace a placeholder line like:

    <figure class="photo"><span>Photo 1</span></figure>

with:

    <img src="images/cart-1.jpg" alt="The beach cart on the sand" />

Tip: keep photos under ~1 MB each so the page loads fast. JPG is best
for photos.
